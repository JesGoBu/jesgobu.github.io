Tax Preparation

For individuals and business owners alike, taxes can seem like a confusing and daunting task. With tax laws changing every year, it can be challenging to keep up. Even for a relatively simple return, it can be easy to miss opportunities for credits and deductions or make small errors that become more significant problems down the line. Frank Accounting and Tax Services prepares your return with sophisticated software and a careful eye for detail.

BookKeeping

Bookkeeping is the backbone of your company’s accounting functions. Your ledger, or “books,” are an ongoing record of every transaction you make. Any time money changes hands, it gets recorded in your ledger, used and referenced in nearly every other aspect of your business’s accounting needs.

Payroll

If your business employs workers, there are a wide variety of laws and regulations that you must conform to. Many of those regulations are in regards to payroll, as employees must be compensated accurately and on time and payroll taxes must be accounted for and paid. Frank Tax and Accounting Services can process your payroll, ensure that your business remains compliant while paying your employees what they’ve earned.

QuickBooks

QuickBooks is a powerful digital tool for the modern entrepreneur. Fully customizable and capable of automating a multitude of functions, QuickBooks can greatly reduce the time you spend on your business’s financial needs. Frank Tax and Accounting Services can help you set up the software and tailor it to your unique requirements. Furthermore, we can train you in its use, and even maintain it on a routine basis.
